HOUSTON TENNIS


Houston Tennis Foundation was built promote the development of tennis among underprivileged children as a means of healthful recreation and physical fitness.

Since its founding it has worked closely with USTA Eastern Section to provide grants and other financial assistance to hundreds of worthy tennis programs and organizations that focus on underserved and at-risk youth and people with disabilities. In that time, JTF has provided over $3 million in grants and scholarships.

JTF also oversees the USTA Eastern Tennis Hall of Fame, which honors players, volunteers and contributors who have impacted tennis in the USTA Eastern Section. Each year, three or four individuals are inducted into the Hall. Proceeds from the annual induction ceremony go to supported programs and scholarships.

"LIFE IS BETTER ON THE COURT"

MISSION

Houston Tennis provides the tools and training for kids who have the passion to play tennis and create a community in a growing foundation in order to grow on and off the court.

VISION

Our vision is to transform Houston communities by making tennis, health and wellness, educational opportunities accessible to at-risk youth.
